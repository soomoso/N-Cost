How does this help you

BE AWARE OF YOUR EARNINGS AND COSTS

For employees

In our user surveys, a significant share of people — around 6 in 10 — say that checking their net salary calculations before accepting a job helps them make better decisions. By clearly showing earnings, deductions, and real take-home pay, our tool gives candidates the confidence to compare offers, understand hidden costs, and choose opportunities that truly match their financial needs.

REDUCE GHOSTING AND RENEGOTIATIONS

For HR and recruitersc

Candidates frequently ghost offers or renegotiate at the last minute once they realize their actual take-home pay is much lower than expected. By sharing a clear, accurate breakdown generated by our calculator (gross → all deductions → net + 13th + vacation bonus), you reduce surprises, speed up the hiring process, and build trust — because the first time they see the real number isn’t on their first payslip.
